/ 05 — FAQ

The small print, spelled out.

Clear, honest answers — no fine print, no runaround.

Do I need to be home?+
Nope. Leave bins at the curb on trash day — we handle the rest and text you a "clean complete" photo when done.
What about winter?+
We pause routes mid‑December through February when temps stay below freezing. Your account is credited — no double‑billing.
How is this different from a garden hose?+
Heat (200°F vs. cold), pressure (3,000 PSI vs. ~40), and capture — our truck collects all greywater so nothing hits your storm drain.
Can I skip a clean?+
Yes — text us 48 hours ahead. We'll skip and bump your next clean. No charge, no drama.
How do I pay?+
Card on file, charged after each clean — never before. See the difference first, then pay.
Can I add a third bin?+
Yes. Every plan covers trash + recycling. Add a yard waste bin (any size) for $5 per clean, set in your account at any time.
How do I cancel?+
Text or call us — no cancellation fee, no forms. We'll confirm by the end of the business day and your card won't be charged again.
What if my trash day changes?+
Just text or call us at (651) 392‑6499. We'll update your route within 24 hours and confirm the new day.
